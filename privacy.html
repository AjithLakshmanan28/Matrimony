Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Vivaha & Co. ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you contact us through WhatsApp, phone or our website, and how we use it to provide matchmaking consultancy services.

2. Information We Collect

When you message us on WhatsApp, call us, or fill our enquiry form, we may collect your name, phone number, age, education, profession, location, religion, community and partner preferences, as voluntarily shared by you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you share to understand your requirements, shortlist suitable matches, coordinate introductions between families, and improve our consultancy services. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Sharing of Information

Your profile details are shared only with families who have expressed mutual interest, and only after your consent. We never publish your information publicly on this website or elsewhere.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information shared with us. As communication primarily happens over WhatsApp and phone, please also review WhatsApp's own privacy policy for how they handle message data.

6. Your Choices

You may request that we stop contacting you or delete your information from our records at any time by messaging us on WhatsApp or calling our office.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, reach us at hello@vivahaandco.com or message us directly on WhatsApp.
